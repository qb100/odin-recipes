Biscuits and Gravy

What are Biscuits and Gravy?

Biscuits and gravy is a popular breakfast dish served in the souther US. The dish consists of biscuits covered in sawmill or meat gravy, made from the drippings of cooked sausage, flour, milk, and bits of sausage, bacon, ground beef, or other meat. The gravy is seasoned with pepper.

Ingredients

  • 1 can refrigerated jumbo buttermilk biscuits
  • 3 tablespoons bacon grease or butter
  • 12 ounces breakfast sausage
  • 1/3 cup flour
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1 cube chicken bouilloon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Instructions

  1. Coook the biscuits in the oven according to the directions on the can
  2. Place a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Crumble the sausage into small pieces and place them into the skillet.
  4. Cook the sausage until no longer pink.
  5. Add the bacon grease and flour, stirring to combine and cook until the mixture is golden brown.
  6. Gradually add the milk along with the seasonings, stirring constantly to prevent lumps.
  7. Simmer uncovered for 10 minutes or until desired thickness, stirring occasionally.
  8. Add salt and pepper to taste.

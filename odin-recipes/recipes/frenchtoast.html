French Toast

What is French Toast?

French toast is a dish made of sliced bread that is soaked in a bath of eggs and milk, and the pan fried. It is usually served as a sweet dish finished off with powdered sugar and syrup, but can be served savory.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of bread: challah, brioche, or white
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl combine the cinnamon, nutmeg, and sugar and set it aside
  2. In a skillet melt butter over medium heat
  3. In a bowl whisk together the cinnamon mixture, eggs, milk, and vanilla.
  4. Pour the mixture into a shallow container large to fit a slice of bread.
  5. Dip a slice of bread into your egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides
  6. Place bread in the skillet, and fry until golden brown, then flip to cook the other side.
  7. Serve with syrup and pwdered sugar.
